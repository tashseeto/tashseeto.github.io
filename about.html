Who Am I?

I’m Tash, a Business Analyst with a background in IT implementation projects beginning my journey into the wonderful world of web and application development.

I love people, data, efficiency, solving problems, and the beauty when all four mix!

I’m a balanced brain individual. A translator between the languages of logic and creativity.

I live data, efficiency, and analytical thinking… which when combined with my intuitive, empathic, and artistic personality; I am able to see innovative solutions from a variety of perspectives.

This year I am excited to expand my creative problem-solving toolkit through the She{Codes} Plus program. Learning how programming languages can bring to life beautiful and functional applications and network with a bunch of likeminded women in Tech.

When I’m not geeking out on code, you can find me immersed in our natural world, spending time with my nearest and dearest or dancing one of the many different styles I enjoy. Like coding, dancing is a language… expression through movement is something I cannot live without.
